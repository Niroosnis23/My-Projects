ABOUT THE FACULTY

The Faculty of Management Studies (FMS) is one of the six faculties which operates as one of the ‘Centre of Excellence’ in management studies. It has a population of around 2500 students following undergraduate studies and a young but energetic team of about 70 members of the academic staff. The faculty offers educational programs both at undergraduate and postgraduate levels; they include Bachelor’s degrees, postgraduate diplomas, postgraduate degrees, and doctoral degrees offered under different sub-disciplines of management. The faculty consists of six academic departments.

HISTORY OF THE FACULTY

Welcome to the Faculty of Management Studies at Rajarata University of Sri Lanka!
As the Dean of the Faculty of Management Studies, I am honored to lead a vibrant community of students, faculty, and staff who are dedicated to excellence in management education, research, and professional development. Our faculty is committed to nurturing the next generation of leaders and equipping them with the knowledge, skills, attitudes and ethical values required to thrive in the dynamic and challenging business landscape.

We strive to create an intellectually stimulating environment where students are encouraged to think critically, engage in scholarly discourse, and develop a global perspective. Our distinguished faculty members, who are experts in their respective fields, bring a wealth of academic knowledge and industry experience to the classroom. They are passionate about mentoring and guiding students to unlock their potential and become future leaders who can make positive changes on society at large.

We are committed to fostering a culture of innovation, entrepreneurship, and social responsibility. We encourage our students to think creatively, embrace new technologies, and explore sustainable business practices. Our aim is to develop leaders who are not only competent professionals but also compassionate individuals committed to making a positive difference in society.
